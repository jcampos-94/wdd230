Explore Carlsbad's Outdoor Delights

Carlsbad offers opportunities for outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and golfing. The city has several parks and nature preserves, including Batiquitos Lagoon, a coastal wetland that provides habitat for a variety of bird species and wildlife. Just like the wholesome fruits and refreshing beverages we celebrate, Carlsbad nourishes both body and soul with its invigorating outdoor adventures.
