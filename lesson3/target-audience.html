Target Audience

We aim to attract and help local businesses, visitors and tourists, local residents, and potential investors.

Personas and Scenarios

Sarah

Background

Sarah is a 35-year-old stay-at-home mom with a bachelor's degree in marketing. She lives in a small town and has a household income of $60,000 thanks to her husband's job but the current economical situation of the country has put that income at risk. She has experience working in marketing for a local small business before starting a family. Sarah is interested in starting her own small business and is looking for resources to help her get started. She primarily uses a laptop or tablet to access the internet so she is likely to access the website using those during the day while her children are at school, and may use her mobile device to check for updates while on the go.

Scenarios
  • Sarah visits the Chamber of Commerce website to find information about local business resources and networking events.
  • Sarah is looking for information on how to start a small business, including legal requirements, funding options, and best practices. She visits the Chamber of Commerce website to find guides and resources on starting a small business.
  • Sarah is interested in connecting with other local small business owners for mentorship and advice. She visits the Chamber of Commerce website to find information about mentorship programs and to sign up.
  • Sarah is considering different locations for her business and is looking for information on the local market and competition. She visits the Chamber of Commerce website to find market research reports and data on local demographics.
  • Sarah is interested in attending a workshop on social media marketing for small businesses. She visits the Chamber of Commerce website to find information on upcoming workshops and to register.

Tom

Background

Tom is a 50-year-old small business owner with a high school education. He lives in a mid-sized city and has a household income of $80,000. He has experience running his own business for over 20 years. Tom is looking for ways to expand his business and connect with other local business owners. He primarily uses a desktop computer to access the internet and is likely to access the website during business hours.

Scenarios
  • Tom visits the Chamber of Commerce website to find information on upcoming networking events and workshops.
  • Tom is interested in finding funding opportunities for his business. He visits the Chamber of Commerce website to find information on available grants and loans.
  • Tom is looking for ways to promote his business to a wider audience. He visits the Chamber of Commerce website to find information on marketing and advertising opportunities.
  • Tom wants to learn more about local regulations and compliance related to his industry. He visits the Chamber of Commerce website to find resources and best practices for compliance.
  • Tom is interested in finding ways to reduce his business's environmental impact. He visits the Chamber of Commerce website to find information on sustainability initiatives and resources.
