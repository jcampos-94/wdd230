Demographic Data about Peru

Population

Peru has a population of approximately 33 million people, making it the third most populous country in South America.

Capital

The capital city of Peru is Lima, which is also the largest city in the country.

Language

The official language of Peru is Spanish. However, there are also several indigenous languages spoken throughout the country, such as Quechua and Aymara.

Ethnicity

  • Mestizos (people of mixed European and indigenous descent)
  • Indigenous groups
  • People of African, Asian, and European ancestry

Economy

Peru has a growing and diverse economy. It is known for its mining and mineral resources, including copper, gold, silver, and zinc. Other important sectors include agriculture, manufacturing, services, and tourism.
